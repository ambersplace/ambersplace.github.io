Hi, I'm an immigrant from Hong Kong, now living in north america, can speak and write Chinese. Here's my best tl;dr why you shouldn't put fake Japanese, fake Chinese, or fake w/e in your game. My perspective is Chinese so the following is more probably relevant for Chinese than Japanese but a lot of the points still stand.

  • We are not an imaginary group, we live among you and are your neighbors.
  • These languages mean things and represent culturally important things for us, sometimes sacred, revered, and personal.
  • This means making up gibberish in anothers' language is rude when done carelessly, and violent when wielded with violent intentions.
  • Reducing our language to gibberish is a part of the way ethnic minorities are de-humanized and "othered" (even when you "do it positively cause you like the vibe")
  • This dehumanization is integral to racial violence, both over the course of the 20th century and more recently as seen by the violence against asian americans after the start of the covid 19 pandemic.
  • This is not a new idea, it has been talked to death. It is more complicated than this, and different people have different takes and lines they draw, but please recognize there is historical context for this conversation.
